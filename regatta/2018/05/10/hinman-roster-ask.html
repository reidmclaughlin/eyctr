Hinman Masters 2018- We need players!

May 10, 2018 • James Appel

Greetings fellow EYCers – summer is here and it’s time to start planning for the Hinman Masters again. The regatta is Friday – Sunday, August 18-20. Thanks for the club for sponsoring us for this event again this and helping us grow team racing at EYC. We are working on solidifying the team and I need to firm up who will be sailing. If you are interested and able to sail, please let me know your interest level –

Looking forward to another great regatta!
